Happy Birthday Saba

17 July 2003

Some souls are rare. Yours is one of them.

A Letter For Saba

Dear Saba,

Today is not just another day. It is the day a beautiful soul entered this world and quietly made it brighter.

May this year bring you peace when life feels heavy, strength when days feel long, and happiness that stays long after today ends.

May every black rose remind you of your uniqueness, every butterfly remind you of your freedom, and every ocean wave remind you that no matter how far you travel, you are deeply valued and remembered.

Happy Birthday. Keep shining. Keep smiling. Keep being wonderfully you.

— Stephen
